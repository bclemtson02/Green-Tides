Research Methods

Green Tides used a rapid, transparent workflow: frame questions, collect public data, clean & join, visualize, and interpret—within a ~5-week window while learning ArcGIS mapping & dashboards. :contentReference[oaicite:0]{index=0}

1) Frame the Questions

  • Poverty trend and level in Imperial Beach.
  • Links between education and income disparity.
  • Crime/safety context.
  • Where inequalities show up across the city. :contentReference[oaicite:1]{index=1}

2) Data Sources

  • DataUSA (demographics, attainment, income). Note: site export required per-race downloads that were manually merged. :contentReference[oaicite:2]{index=2}
  • Data Commons (poverty series for Imperial Beach). :contentReference[oaicite:3]{index=3}
  • Additional cited sources (county/national comparisons, crime context) as documented in the report. :contentReference[oaicite:4]{index=4}

3) Cleaning & Integration

  • Downloaded DataUSA attainment by race (White, Black, Two+), added a race column, and combined to one table. :contentReference[oaicite:5]{index=5}
  • Aligned indicator names/years; joined to city-level series (e.g., poverty). :contentReference[oaicite:6]{index=6}

4) Analysis & Visualization

  • Mapped median income and attainment patterns; used ArcGIS for maps/dashboards. :contentReference[oaicite:7]{index=7}
  • Compared IB to county & national benchmarks; summarized disparities. :contentReference[oaicite:8]{index=8}

5) Interpretation

Findings link education gaps to income differences and highlight inequality (SDG 10) as the central challenge, with poverty and attainment below county averages. :contentReference[oaicite:9]{index=9}

Scope, Limits & Ethics

  • Time-boxed to ~5 weeks; relied on existing datasets (no interviews/fieldwork). :contentReference[oaicite:10]{index=10}
  • Some sources require manual steps (possible transcription error risk). :contentReference[oaicite:11]{index=11}
  • City-level stats can mask neighborhood variation; interpret accordingly. :contentReference[oaicite:12]{index=12}
